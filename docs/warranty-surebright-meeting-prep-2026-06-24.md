## 3. Recommended LILA Warranty Policy (to ratify before/at this meeting)

This is what VCycene's warranty should say once formalized. Propose this structure and ask Surebright to match it in their plan terms.

### 3-Year Manufacturer Warranty (VCycene-owned)
**Covered:**
- Mechanical failure: motor, gearbox, agitator, heating element, fan
- Electrical failure: PCB, sensors, display, wiring harness, power supply
- Software/firmware defects causing malfunction (distinguishable from user error)
- Structural failure of the housing under normal operating conditions
- Seals, gaskets, and fasteners if they fail under normal use (not wear)

**Not covered:**
- Accidental damage (drops, spills, fire, flood)
- Normal wear items: carbon filters, odor media, consumable bio-accelerant
- Damage from unauthorized modifications or repairs
- Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents) not affecting function
- Failure caused by operating outside rated conditions (wrong voltage, outdoor exposure)
- Commercial or industrial use beyond intended residential/small-business scope

**Claim process (proposed):**
1. Customer emails support@lilacomposter.com or submits via Help Center
2. Support team requests proof of purchase + photo/video of defect
3. VCycene ships replacement part or unit within [X business days]
4. Defective unit returned at VCycene's cost (prepaid label)

### Extended Warranty — Years 4–6 (Surebright-powered, customer purchase)
- Covers same mechanical/electrical failures as manufacturer warranty
- Surebright adjudicates; VCycene not involved in claims
- Term options to discuss: +1 year, +2 years, +3 years

### Accidental Damage (optional add-on, Surebright)
- Covers drops, liquid spills, and unforeseen physical damage
- Combined with Extended Warranty or standalone from purchase date

## 5. Clarification Questions for Surebright (Soumya)

**Coverage & terms**
1. Does Surebright's warranty activate from **day of purchase** and run alongside our 3-year manufacturer warranty, or does it only kick in **after** our warranty expires? If both active, who adjudicates during years 1–3?
2. For the LILA Composter specifically: are **motor, heating element, and PCB failures** explicitly covered under mechanical/electrical? Can you confirm in writing before we sign?
3. Are **software and firmware defects** (e.g., firmware update breaks composting cycle) covered, or is software excluded?
4. What are the **standard exclusions** for a kitchen appliance in your plan template? Can we review the exact T&Cs customers will see before going live?
5. How does coverage work for **consumable components** (carbon filters, bio-accelerant media) — are these explicitly excluded?

**Claims & operations**
6. What is your **claim adjudication SLA** — how many days from filing to resolution decision?
7. For a repair outcome: is the customer shipping the unit to **VCycene** for repair, or to a third-party service center? VCycene would need to know if we're the repair fulfillment point.
8. For a **replacement** outcome: who ships the replacement unit — Surebright from inventory, or VCycene? And who holds the replacement inventory?
9. Can VCycene see **individual claim details** (customer name, defect description, outcome) in the merchant dashboard for quality/QC purposes?
10. If a customer's claim is **denied**, does Surebright communicate the denial, or does it come back to VCycene's support team?

**Business & compliance**
11. What is the **actual revenue share percentage** for a home appliance in our volume range?
12. What is our **minimum revenue threshold requirement** — does VCycene qualify today? If not, what's the path?
13. VCycene's customer base is ~50% Canadian. Are there any differences in **PIPEDA** compliance handling vs. US CCPA? Who is the data controller for Canadian customers?
14. What happens to **active customer warranties** if either party terminates the agreement? Are those warranties honored by the underwriter regardless?
15. Your Terms of Service state merchants must "process refunds at their own cost" when required by law. In practice, when does this come up — and how often?

**Integration**
16. Does Surebright provide a **merchant-facing API or webhook** that we can use to sync claim events into our internal system (MakeLila)? (We want warranty claim status visible to our support team without manual lookup.)
17. How long does technical integration take for a Shopify store already using Klaviyo for post-purchase emails?
