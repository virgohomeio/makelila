# LILA Composter — Warranty Policy
**Version:** 1.0 · June 2026 · VCycene Inc.

---

## 3-Year Manufacturer Warranty

Every LILA Composter is covered by a **3-year manufacturer warranty** from the original date of purchase.

### What's Covered

**Mechanical & Electrical Components**
- Motor and gearbox
- Heating element
- PCB (main control board)
- Fan and airflow system
- Sensors and display
- Wiring harness and power supply

**Software & Firmware**
- Firmware defects that cause the unit to malfunction
- OTA update failures that prevent normal operation

**Structural**
- Housing and enclosure structural failure under normal operating conditions
- Seals, gaskets, and fasteners that fail under normal use

### What's Not Covered

- Accidental damage (drops, spills, physical impact)
- Normal wear items: carbon filters, odour media, bio-accelerant consumables
- Damage from unauthorized modifications or third-party repairs
- Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents) that does not affect function
- Failure caused by operating outside rated conditions (incorrect voltage, outdoor exposure, submersion)
- Commercial or industrial use beyond the intended residential/small-business scope
- Damage caused by acts of nature (fire, flood)

### How to Make a Claim

1. Email **support@lilacomposter.com** or submit via the Help Center
2. Include your order number and a short description of the issue
3. Our team will respond within 1–2 business days to request photo or video evidence if needed
4. We'll confirm the outcome (replacement part, unit swap, or repair) and ship your resolution
5. If a unit return is required, we'll send a prepaid return label

### How Extended Warranty Claims Work

Extended warranty claims are handled entirely by Surebright — VCycene is not involved in adjudication.

1. File a claim at **customer.surebright.com** or contact Surebright support (support@surebright.com · 855-306-4874)
2. Surebright reviews the claim against your plan terms
3. Valid claims are resolved by repair, unit replacement, or refund — return shipping is covered
4. Claim status is visible to VCycene's support team internally for tracking purposes

### Purchasing an Extended Warranty

Extended warranties are available at checkout on lilacomposter.com or within **30 days of your original purchase**. Coverage terms and pricing are displayed at time of purchase.
