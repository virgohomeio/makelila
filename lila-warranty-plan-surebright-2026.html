Claims Flow
Two parallel paths depending on which tier the claim falls under. The customer experience differs significantly post-year-3.
Years 1–3 · VCycene
Manufacturer Warranty Claim
Handled by VCycene support team
1
Customer contacts support
Email support@lilacomposter.com or Help Center form
2
Support team verifies
Requests proof of purchase + photo or video of defect
3
Outcome determined
Replacement part, full unit swap, or repair. MakeLila ticket created.
4
VCycene ships resolution
Prepaid return label issued for defective unit. Replacement ships within agreed SLA.
5
Tracked in MakeLila
Service ticket + PostShipment replacement record logged. Linked to customer & serial.
Years 4+ · Surebright
Extended Warranty Claim
Handled by Surebright — VCycene not involved in adjudication
1
Customer files online
Self-serve at customer.surebright.com or via support@surebright.com · (855) 306-4874
2
Surebright adjudicates
Claim reviewed against warranty terms. Target SLA: TBC on call (99% resolved in 24hr per their docs)
3
Outcome: repair / replace / refund
Shipping for repairs covered by Surebright. If replacement: who ships? — to confirm.
4
MakeLila synced via webhook
Claim status pushed to warranty_claims table. Visible in PostShipment → Warranty Claims tab. Service ticket auto-linked if physical repair needed.
5
VCycene earns revenue share
Surebright invoices periodically. Net revenue added to Finance module reporting.

Questions for Surebright (Soumya)
Prioritized for a 30-minute call. Coverage and claims questions first — business terms second.
Coverage & Terms
1
Coverage Structure
Does Surebright coverage activate from day of purchase and run alongside our 3-year manufacturer warranty — or only after it expires? If both are active in years 1–3, who adjudicates and who has final say?
2
Product-Specific Scope
For the LILA Composter specifically: are motor, heating element, and PCB failures explicitly listed under mechanical/electrical coverage in your plan template for kitchen appliances? Can we review the exact customer-facing T&Cs before go-live?
3
Software Coverage
Are software/firmware defects covered — e.g., a firmware update that corrupts the composting cycle controller? Or is software explicitly excluded from the mechanical/electrical plan?
4
Exclusions List
What are the standard exclusions for a kitchen composting appliance in your plan template? Specifically: are carbon filters, odour media, and bio-accelerant consumables explicitly excluded?
Claims & Operations
5
Adjudication SLA
What is your claim adjudication SLA — days from filing to resolution decision? Your docs say 99% in 24hrs — is that the written SLA or a performance metric?
6
Repair Logistics
For a repair outcome: does the customer ship the unit to VCycene for repair, or to a Surebright-network service center? VCycene needs to know if we're the designated repair point and plan capacity accordingly.
7
Replacement Fulfillment
For a replacement outcome: who ships the unit — Surebright from held inventory, or VCycene from our stock? If VCycene ships, how are we notified and reimbursed?
8
Merchant Claim Visibility
Can VCycene see individual claim details (customer, defect description, outcome) in the merchant dashboard for QC purposes — or only aggregate metrics?
9
Denied Claims
If a claim is denied: does Surebright communicate the denial to the customer, or does it route back to VCycene's support team?
Business & Compliance
10
Revenue Share
What is the revenue share % for a home appliance at our volume? We'd like this in writing before the next step.
11
Contract Exit & Continuity
If either party terminates the agreement, are active customer warranties honored by the underwriter regardless? This is a hard requirement — we won't leave customers without coverage they paid for.
12
Canadian Compliance
~50% of LILA customers are Canadian. How does PIPEDA compliance work for Canadian customers — who is the data controller, and how do you handle provincial differences (Quebec Law 25)?
Integration & Technical
13
Merchant API / Webhook
Do you provide a merchant-facing API or webhook that pushes claim status events (filed, approved, denied, closed)? We want warranty claim visibility in our internal ops system without manual lookup in your dashboard.
14
Klaviyo Integration
We use Klaviyo for post-purchase flows. How does Surebright's warranty upsell integrate with our existing Klaviyo sequences — does it need to be turned off during the Surebright post-purchase window?
